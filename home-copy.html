Meet The Team

We are an e-commerce retail pop and mom business for the family, located in Los Angeles, CA.  We are dedicated to providing an Online shopping that is easy and convenient at amazing prices.  With a wide array of products ranging from Household Supplies to Office Supplies to Toys, Health & Beauty products and Montessori products, FamilyStop is a one-stop-shop for the family's lifestyle in the 21st century. We source our products from major U.S. wholesalers and manufacturers, utilizing our amazing network of suppliers and logistics.
